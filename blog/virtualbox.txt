Ubuntu 14.04.2 VirtualBox Resolution

1. Go to http://download.virtualbox.org/virtualbox/
2. Find the exact version you have of virtualbox
3. Download VBoxGuestAdditions_version.iso and plug it into your VM (will probably be located somewhere like C:\Program Files\Oracle\VirtualBox )
4. From Devices, in virtualbox, click on insert guest additions, and insert the new iso.
5. Run the iso, then restart VM and you should be good to go
